# Glossary

A comprehensive list of terms used throughout the Humanoid Robotics textbook.

## A

**Actuator**: A component of a robot that converts energy into motion.

**AI Integration**: Incorporating artificial intelligence techniques into robotic systems.

## C

**Control Systems**: Systems that manage and regulate the behavior of robotic mechanisms.

## D

**Dynamics**: The study of forces and torques that cause motion in robotic systems.

## K

**Kinematics**: The study of motion without considering the forces that cause it.

## L

**Locomotion**: The ability to move from one place to another.

## M

**Manipulation**: The ability to physically interact with and control objects.

## P

**Perception**: The ability to sense and interpret the environment.

## S

**Sensors**: Devices that detect and respond to physical inputs from the environment.
