# Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the comprehensive AI-driven textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics.

## About This Book

This textbook is designed to provide comprehensive coverage of humanoid robotics concepts, from foundational principles to advanced implementations. It serves multiple audiences:

- **Students**: Learn fundamental and advanced concepts in humanoid robotics
- **Educators**: Use this content for course materials and curriculum development
- **Content Creators**: Contribute and update content following standardized guidelines

## How to Use This Book

The textbook is organized into chapters that cover essential topics in humanoid robotics:

- Start with the foundations and build your knowledge progressively
- Each chapter includes concepts, examples, diagrams, and exercises
- Complete exercises to test your understanding
- Use the search function to find specific topics across all chapters

## Chapters

1. **Foundations of Humanoid Robotics** - Core concepts and principles
2. **Kinematics** - Movement and positioning systems
3. **Dynamics** - Forces and motion in robotic systems
4. **Actuators & Motors** - Power and control systems
5. **Sensors** - Perception and feedback systems
6. **Control Systems** - Managing robotic behavior
7. **Motion Planning** - Pathfinding and navigation
8. **Locomotion & Gait** - Walking and movement patterns
9. **Perception** - Understanding the environment
10. **Manipulation** - Interaction with objects
11. **Simulation** - Modeling and testing environments
12. **Safety** - Ensuring safe robotic operation
13. **AI Integration** - Intelligence and learning in robotics

## Features

- Each chapter includes exercises with automated validation
- Interactive diagrams and visualizations
- Real-world examples and case studies
- Standardized format for consistency
